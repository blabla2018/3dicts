What This App Does

This app is a dictionary comparison tool. It shows the same English word from Oxford, Cambridge, and Longman in one interface so you can compare definitions, examples, pronunciation, and usage quickly.

How Search Works

  • Search once and open the same word in all three dictionaries.
  • Recent words are stored locally in your browser.
  • Suggestions appear while you type.

Mobile

  • One dictionary is shown at a time.
  • Use horizontal swipes to switch dictionaries.
  • Use the floating search button to open full-screen search.

Desktop

  • Wide window: all three dictionaries are visible at once.
  • Narrow window: one dictionary is visible at a time.
